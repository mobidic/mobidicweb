About MoBiDiC

What is it?

MoBiDiC is the French acronym for Montpellier Bioinformatics for Clinical Diagnosis.
It is a small group of bioinformaticians working at the University Hospital in Montpellier, France, and, as suggested by the name, working in Molecular Diagnostics.
Our Development skills include NGS pipelines, databases design and implementation, GUIs development...
